About

    I am very new to the world of web development and computer programming in general. I received my Bachelor's in Music Performance (Voice) from the University of Michigan in 2013. Since then, I've had odd jobs to sustain me while I search for my passion.
    During that time, I've held many different jobs: waiter, Uber driver, laser tag manufacturer, associate for an Amazon fulfilment center. I've learned a little bit about myself and what I find fulfilling in every job I've taken. Most recently, I am working as a substitute teacher and finding it very rewarding. It has been especially rewarding to teach music to the elementary kids.
    But as far as a career goes, I am very glad I found the Coding Bootcamp program at SMU. I'm finally getting that intellectual, problem solving stimulation I've been yearning for all these years. I've always loved STEM; even in college I took as many music theory courses as I could (the study of how music works and is put together). I'm very glad to be doing something like this. If I can marry it with my other passion -- helping others and enriching my community -- I will be on cloud nine.
